# Pace

**Harm reduction for nights out.**

Pace is a quiet companion for the nights you choose to drink. It estimates your BAC, helps you plan your ride home before the first drink, and gives you tools to look out for yourself and the people around you.

It's not a party tracker. It's not a competition. It's the app you keep open at 1am because it makes the night easier — not because it's nagging you.

---

## What it does

- **Tonight tab** — log drinks, see live BAC, watch your sober-by time tick down
- **Safety tab** — pick a ride plan before the first drink; one-tap Uber, Lyft, transit, or an emergency contact, with your home address already loaded as the destination
- **Live Activity** — current BAC, ride plan, and sober-by time on your lock screen and in the Dynamic Island, without unlocking your phone
- **Sleep-aware estimates** — if you slept under six hours, Pace adjusts your BAC estimate upward by 15% to reflect how fatigue compounds alcohol
- **Week tab** — your drink history, computed locally

## What it doesn't do

- No accounts. No servers. No analytics.
- No ads. No in-app purchases.
- No data ever leaves your device.
